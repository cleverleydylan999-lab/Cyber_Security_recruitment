Job Overview

We're seeking an experienced Penetration Tester to lead our offensive security operations. In this role, you'll conduct comprehensive security assessments of our clients' infrastructure, identifying and documenting critical vulnerabilities before malicious actors can exploit them. You'll work on diverse projects ranging from network infrastructure to web applications, cloud environments, and physical security assessments.

Key Responsibilities

  • Conduct authorized penetration tests on client infrastructure including networks, applications, and physical locations
  • Develop custom exploitation techniques and tools to identify security weaknesses
  • Document findings in comprehensive reports with executive summaries and technical details
  • Mentor junior penetration testers and share advanced techniques with the security team
  • Stay current with the latest attack methodologies and security vulnerabilities
  • Collaborate with clients to prioritize remediation efforts based on risk assessment
  • Testify as an expert witness in legal proceedings when required
  • Develop and maintain penetration testing frameworks and methodologies

Required Qualifications

  • 7+ years of cybersecurity experience, with at least 4 years in penetration testing
  • Deep knowledge of network architecture, TCP/IP protocols, and common vulnerabilities
  • Expert-level proficiency in penetration testing tools (Burp Suite, Metasploit, Nmap, etc.)
  • Strong programming skills in Python, Bash, or similar languages
  • Experience with both manual and automated vulnerability assessment techniques
  • Understanding of cryptography, authentication mechanisms, and encryption protocols
  • Excellent communication skills for technical and non-technical audiences
  • OSCP, CEH, or similar certifications required

Preferred Qualifications

  • GPEN or OSCE certification
  • Experience with cloud security assessments (AWS, Azure, GCP)
  • Knowledge of mobile app security testing
  • Experience with API security assessments
  • Red team or advanced adversary simulation experience
