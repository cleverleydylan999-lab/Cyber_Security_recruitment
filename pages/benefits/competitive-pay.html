Equity & Stock Options

Become a stakeholder in CyberShield's success:

Stock Options

All employees receive stock options in our company. Your success is our success. Options vest over 4 years with a 1-year cliff, ensuring long-term commitment and value appreciation.

Significant Grants

Entry-level roles: 500-2,000 options. Senior roles: 5,000-25,000+ options. Directors and principals can receive even larger grants depending on responsibility.

Periodic Refreshes

Additional option grants every 2-3 years based on performance and tenure, ensuring you continue to benefit from company growth.

Protected Value

Acceleration on change of control provisions protect your equity interests in case of acquisition or major restructuring events.
