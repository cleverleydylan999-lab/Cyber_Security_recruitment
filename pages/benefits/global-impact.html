Real-World Impact Stories

How our team makes a difference:

01

Preventing Hospital Ransomware

Our incident response team detected and stopped a ransomware attack targeting 3 major hospitals, preventing critical patient care systems from being compromised and saving potentially thousands of lives.

02

Stopping Financial Fraud Ring

Our threat intelligence team identified an international fraud operation attempting to steal $500M+ from financial institutions. Our investigation led to law enforcement collaboration and arrests across 5 countries.

03

Protecting Student Data

We secured the educational records of 500,000+ students across multiple universities, implementing security improvements that became industry standards for academic institutions.

04

Critical Infrastructure Defense

Our infrastructure security specialists hardened the security posture of electric grid operators, protecting power delivery to 2+ million households and businesses.

05

Supply Chain Security

We discovered and disclosed vulnerabilities in software used by millions. Our responsible disclosure prevented potential global supply chain attacks affecting enterprises worldwide.

06

Disaster Recovery Support

After a major natural disaster, our team provided emergency cybersecurity support to critical relief organizations, ensuring aid delivery systems remained operational.

Types of Global Impact Work
