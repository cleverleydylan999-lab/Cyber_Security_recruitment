Job Overview

We're seeking a Compliance Officer to develop, implement, and maintain our security compliance programs. You'll ensure our organization meets regulatory requirements including GDPR, HIPAA, PCI-DSS, ISO 27001, and SOC 2. In this role, you'll conduct audits, manage security policies, coordinate with external auditors, and drive continuous improvement of our compliance posture.

Key Responsibilities

  • Develop and maintain security policies and compliance frameworks
  • Ensure adherence to regulatory requirements (GDPR, HIPAA, PCI-DSS, ISO 27001, SOC 2)
  • Conduct internal audits and assessments of compliance controls
  • Prepare for external audits and manage auditor relationships
  • Develop and deliver compliance training to employees
  • Maintain compliance documentation and evidence repositories
  • Monitor regulatory changes and assess impact on organization
  • Manage risk assessment and management processes
  • Coordinate security incident reporting and remediation
  • Work with legal and engineering teams on compliance-related projects

Required Qualifications

  • 4-6 years of experience in cybersecurity compliance or audit
  • Deep knowledge of GDPR, HIPAA, PCI-DSS, and ISO 27001
  • Experience with SOC 2 Type II compliance
  • Proficiency with compliance management tools and frameworks
  • Strong understanding of risk management methodologies
  • Experience writing and maintaining security policies
  • Excellent communication and documentation skills
  • CISSP, CISM, or CCSK certification

Preferred Qualifications

  • Experience with compliance automation tools
  • Knowledge of GRC (Governance, Risk, and Compliance) platforms
  • Background in internal audit or external audit
  • Experience with data privacy assessments
  • Knowledge of Canadian or regional privacy laws
