Job Overview

Join our Security Operations Center (SOC) as a Security Analyst where you'll be the frontline defense against cyber threats. You'll monitor, detect, and respond to security incidents across our client infrastructure 24/7. This role combines technical expertise with problem-solving skills as you analyze security alerts, investigate potential breaches, and coordinate incident response efforts. You'll work with cutting-edge SIEM platforms and contribute to the continuous improvement of our security operations.

Key Responsibilities

  • Monitor security events and alerts in real-time using SIEM platforms (Splunk, ELK, etc.)
  • Investigate security incidents and determine appropriate response measures
  • Perform threat hunting to identify indicators of compromise and malicious activity
  • Analyze logs from various security tools (firewalls, IDS/IPS, endpoint protection)
  • Document incident findings and create comprehensive incident reports
  • Collaborate with incident response team during active security incidents
  • Perform malware analysis and reverse engineering of suspicious files
  • Maintain and update incident response runbooks and procedures
  • Participate in tabletop exercises and incident response drills
  • Keep up-to-date with emerging threats and security trends

Required Qualifications

  • 3-5 years of cybersecurity experience with focus on SOC or incident response
  • Experience with SIEM platforms (Splunk, Elasticsearch, Sumologic, etc.)
  • Strong knowledge of network protocols, TCP/IP, and network architecture
  • Proficiency with log analysis and data correlation techniques
  • Understanding of common attack vectors and threat intelligence
  • Experience with threat hunting and adversary behavior analysis
  • Familiarity with incident response procedures and frameworks (NIST, MITRE ATT&CK)
  • Security+ or CISSP certification (or equivalent)

Preferred Qualifications

  • CEH or GCIH certification
  • Experience with endpoint detection and response (EDR) tools
  • Basic scripting skills (Python, PowerShell, Bash)
  • Knowledge of malware analysis and reverse engineering
  • Experience with MISP or other threat intelligence platforms
  • Active security clearance or ability to obtain one
