Job Overview

Join our Security Operations Center (SOC) as a Technician and launch your cybersecurity career! You'll support our 24/7 security monitoring operations, assist with ticket management, perform routine security checks, and learn from experienced security professionals. This is an excellent opportunity to gain hands-on experience in security operations and grow your technical skills under mentorship.

Key Responsibilities

  • Monitor security alerts and create incident tickets
  • Perform basic log analysis and trend identification
  • Assist with vulnerability scanning and remediation tracking
  • Support network and security tool administration
  • Contribute to security documentation and runbooks
  • Participate in incident response activities when needed
  • Conduct routine security checks and compliance verifications
  • Maintain incident management systems and ticketing queues
  • Support security awareness and training initiatives
  • Assist with patch management and system updates

Required Qualifications

  • High school diploma or equivalent (bachelor's degree preferred)
  • 0-2 years of IT or security operations experience
  • Basic understanding of networking and cybersecurity concepts
  • Familiarity with Windows and Linux operating systems
  • Basic knowledge of TCP/IP and network protocols
  • Excellent attention to detail and ability to follow procedures
  • Strong written and verbal communication skills
  • Willingness to work 24/7 shift rotations

Preferred Qualifications

  • CompTIA A+ or Network+ certification
  • Experience with SIEM platforms or log management
  • Familiarity with common security tools
  • Basic scripting knowledge (Python, PowerShell, Bash)
  • Previous helpdesk or technical support experience
